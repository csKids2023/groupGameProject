Game Instructions

City Undercover

Game Description

This game is a cop roleplay game, where you choose your own adventure by picking different options to continue the story line.

Story

Inspector Daniel Harris, a dedicated and by-the-book cop, has just received a promotion within the precinct. He's well-liked by his colleagues, but not everyone is thrilled about his ascent. Officer Max Inferno, a corrupt and ambitious cop, resents Harris's success and secretly plots to undermine him.

Characters

Protagonists:
Inspector Daniel Harris

A brave and noble cop, committed to justice. He began his career as an investigator after graduating from top college.

Antagonist:
Officer Max Inferno

An evil and corrupt person that lacks morals. Envies Daniel's success. Graduated behind Daniel and is now working in an organization, TITANIUM.

Officer's Partner:
Detective Mike Sullivan

Loyal friend, ambitious and willing to do anything for his friend, Daniel. They have been friends since kindergarten.

Chief:
Mr. James Cooper

Serious but friendly chief who is dedicated to stopping injustice, but is very stubborn. Has worked for the city for 20 years and has lots of experience.

President:
Benjamin Foster

A confident and well liked president. Very important role.

Rich Friend:
Thomas Whiteman

A kind man and very charitable. Willing to help out his life long friend Daniel Harris.

Mom:
Karren Harris

Mother of Daniel and cares deeply about her son. She is scared to see Daniek get caught into trouble.
